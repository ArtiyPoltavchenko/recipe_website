### Ingredients
- 500g beef
- 2 carrots
- 3 potatoes
- 1 onion
- 500ml beef stock

### Instructions
1. Cut the beef into chunks.
2. Peel and chop the vegetables.
3. Heat oil in a pot and sear the beef.
4. Add vegetables and cook for 5 minutes.
5. Pour in the beef stock and simmer for 2 hours.
